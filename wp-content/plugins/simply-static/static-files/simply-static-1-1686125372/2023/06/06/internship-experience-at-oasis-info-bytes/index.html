Internship Experience at Oasis Info bytes

blogaccount June 6, 2023 3 min read

About Me…

Hello, Everyone my self Shubham Mahajan. I am a 2nd year student of Parul Institute of Technology and pursuing my graduation in Computer Science with specialization in Artificial Intelligence. I am pursing Artificial Intelligence but still I am very interested in Web Development field. In web Development I want to become a Full Stack Developer Who is the combination of Front End Developer as well as Back End Developer. In this blog i am going to discussing about my internship experience in web development at Oasis Info byte company.

About Company

Oasis Infobyte

 Bytes is software company situated in Satya Niketan, South West New Delhi. Oasis Info Bytes Develop Personalized Software At Our Company, They Turned The Digital Dreams Of Our Clients Into A Reality. They Believes In Bridging The Gap Between Students And Their Knowledge In The Industrial Field & Bringing Them A Step Closer To Their Dream.

In Oasis Info bytes company team members are very Polite in behavior as well as very helpful. I am glad to found the mentors like Adarsh Prakash Sir who is Web Developer And Designer, Lakshya Dutta who is Animation Designer not only this Nancy Dhir mam who is from Human Resource.


Internship Domains Available in Company

Oasis Info bytes company provides internship in many different fields of Computer Science and Technology. Company provides :-

  1. Web Development Internship
  2. Graphic Design Internship
  3. Internship in Java Programming
  4. Android Development Internship
  5. Data Science Internships
  6. Cloud Computing Internships

About my Internship task & Experience

I enrolled for Web development Internship, as i came to know that Oasis info bytes internship opportunity is available. I provided my all details and registered for web development internship.

After 10–15 days i got an email from Oasis Info bytes that you have registered succesfully and check your offer letter. I read entire letter very carefully and observed internship task & deadline. In this internship i had to clear any one level among 3 levels available in web development intern and perform all the three task inside selected level. This was a 1 month Virtual unpaid Internship. We had to complete the tasks and upload them on “git hub” in terms of Code and upload on “LinkedIn” in terms of tag for Oasis Info bytes.

Task Submission and Internship Completion

I completed all my task during learning process in my domain of Web development and i uploaded them on “ git hub” for code evidence and posted on “LinkedIn” and tag to company for showing the completion of each task. By competing all task and level i completed my first Internship.



Acknowledgement

I am very glad to inform this that I have completed my first Internship. I am very thankful Oasis Info bytes company for providing me this internship in my interested domain — “Web Development”. While my task performace i was learning that how we have to work in companies exactly after joining. This internship helpled me alot for learning and developing with the technology.
